I started reading this book because my therapist [[Dominique Clement]] suggested that I may have traits of someone of HPI. I see some commonalities to traits of geniuses ([[Geniuses and divergent thinking]]). I'm not sure because I don't feel comfortable. She highlights that there's a difference between having potential, versus developing talent.

[[2023-02-08 Wed]] I finished the book. It took me maybe 6 hours of reading to go through 180 pages or so along with dense notes. I think it was a pretty good intro the field.
