# Informational Cherry-picking

#status/postponed

Related to [[Come As You Are by Emily Nagoski]]

---

Informational cherry picking is a bad idea in science and moralism but maybe not so when it comes to choosing intrinsic personal truths such as our sexuality.

Each of us are quite different, and we will respond to different saliency of information based on our upbringing and prior beliefs.
